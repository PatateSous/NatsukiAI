Meet NatsukiAI ! My osu! playing AI/Bot

NatsukiAI is a big project I built for fun: a bot that plays osu!, i generate scores from her, and I upload them to YouTube. She's experimental, sometimes miss, and always trying to improve her pp gain. This site is a little home for her, show off her best plays, link to the channel, and celebrate the cute chaos.

Why Natsuki?

Because i've loved Natsuki since forever, and i don't see why not >.< (best ddlc girl)

How scores are posted

I record her scores, upload to YouTube, and manually upload the scores here. PP values are tracked and updated with each new upload. (if i remember, i'm a little sillyhead)
